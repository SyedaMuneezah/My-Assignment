Beaf Karahi

 

Beef Karahi is a popular South Asian dish known for its rich and aromatic flavors. Tender pieces of beef are cooked in a wok-like karahi pan with a blend of spices, including cumin, coriander, and ginger-garlic paste. The dish is infused with tomatoes and green chilies, giving it a perfect balance of tanginess and heat. Garnished with fresh coriander, Beef Karahi is a savory delight enjoyed with naan or rice, making it a beloved choice in Pakistani and Indian cuisine.
