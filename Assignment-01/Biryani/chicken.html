Chicken Biryani

 

Chicken biryani is a flavorful and aromatic South Asian rice dish made with basmati rice, tender pieces of chicken, and a blend of fragrant spices. The dish is characterized by its vibrant colors and rich taste, achieved through the careful layering of marinated chicken, partially cooked rice, and aromatic spices like cumin, cardamom, and cloves. The ingredients are cooked together in a pot, allowing the flavors to meld, and the dish is often garnished with fried onions and fresh herbs for an added burst of flavor. Chicken biryani is a beloved and iconic dish, known for its enticing aroma and deliciously complex taste.
