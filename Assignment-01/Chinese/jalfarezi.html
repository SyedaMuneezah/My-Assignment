Jalfarezi with Rice

 

Jalfrezi is a flavorful and spicy South Asian curry dish that originated in the Indian subcontinent. Known for its vibrant taste, it typically features marinated meat, such as chicken or lamb, stir-fried with a variety of colorful vegetables like bell peppers, onions, and tomatoes. The dish is characterized by the use of aromatic spices, including cumin, coriander, and chili, creating a bold and zesty flavor profile. Jalfrezi is often enjoyed with rice or flatbreads, making it a popular choice for those who appreciate a hearty and aromatic curry with a kick of heat.
