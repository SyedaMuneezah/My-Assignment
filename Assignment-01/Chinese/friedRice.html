Fried Rice

 

Fried rice is a flavorful and versatile dish that originates from Asian cuisine. Cooked grains of rice are stir-fried in a wok or skillet with a medley of ingredients such as vegetables, eggs, and a choice of proteins like chicken, shrimp, or tofu. The dish is seasoned with soy sauce and often incorporates garlic and ginger for added depth of flavor. Fried rice is known for its satisfying texture, combination of savory elements, and the ability to easily customize to personal taste preferences. It is a popular and delicious staple in many cultures worldwide.
